VITAL MESSAGE!

Computer Battlegames - 2

You are a laser communications operator. Your job is to intercept robot messages and relay them to Command HQ. A vital code message is expected.

If you relay it correctly, the Robot attack will be crushed, otherwise it's over for the HQ!

RULES:

  • This game tests your skill at remembering a group of letters
  • The computer will ask you for a difficulty from 4 to 10
  • When you have typed in your answer, letters will appear on the screen
  • Memorize them and then type them into the computer and see if you were right
  • Open up the JS console before starting the game!
