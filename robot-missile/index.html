ROBOT MISSILE!

Computer Battlegames - 1

In this game is the year 2582 and the people of SuperEarth are int he midst of battle against the Automaton. A lethal Automaton Missile has just landed and everyone is depending on you to find the secret code which unlocks its defuse mechanism.

If you fail, the entire SuperEarth Command Headquarters will be blown up!

RULES:

  • Your computer knows what the code is
  • You must type in your guess and the computer will tell you if the correct code is higher or lower on the alphabet
  • Your guesses must always be a single letter!
  • You have 10 chances to find the correct secred code before the missile blows up
